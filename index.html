About the Department

Leading the future of data-driven innovation

The Department of Data Science at GM University is at the forefront of data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence education. Established in 2018, our department has quickly become a center of excellence for data science education and research.

We offer comprehensive programs that blend theoretical foundations with practical applications, preparing students for careers in the rapidly evolving field of data science. Our curriculum is designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure relevance and employability.

Message from Head of Department

Dear Students, Welcome to the Department of Data Science at GM University, Davangere — a hub where knowledge meets innovation and data transforms into decisions. In today’s digital world, data is often called the “new oil.” At our department, we go beyond this idea — we believe data is the new intelligence that fuels discovery, research, and progress. We prepare our students to become innovators, problem-solvers, and visionaries in fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, and Cloud Computing. Our teaching philosophy is simple: Learn, Apply, and Innovate. We blend rigorous academics with real-world applications through state-of-the-art laboratories, industry collaborations, hackathons, and research projects. Every student is encouraged to explore, experiment, and engineer solutions that can impact healthcare, finance, agriculture, business, and society at large. We are not just building data professionals; we are nurturing data leaders who carry ethical values, creativity, and a spirit of lifelong learning. I invite you to join us on this exciting journey where curiosity meets creativity, and ideas evolve into innovations that can shape a smarter, more sustainable future.

Mr. Firoz Khan

Head of Department, Data Science
15+ years of experience in Data Science and Machine Learning

Vision

To emerge as a global center of excellence in Data Science, inspiring innovation, research, and ethical practices that transform raw data into intelligence for societal growth and sustainable development.

Missions

  • To deliver cutting-edge education in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Analytics with a balance of theory and real-world applications.
  • To cultivate analytical thinkers and innovators capable of solving complex problems using data-driven approaches.
  • To foster research, entrepreneurship, and industry partnerships that accelerate technological advancements.
  • To empower students with digital leadership skills, creativity, and global perspectives in data science.
  • To nurture responsible professionals who apply data insights ethically for societal well-being and national development.

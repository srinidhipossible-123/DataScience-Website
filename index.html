About the Department

Leading the future of data-driven innovation

The Department of Data Science at GM University is at the forefront of data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence education. Established in 2018, our department has quickly become a center of excellence for data science education and research.

We offer comprehensive programs that blend theoretical foundations with practical applications, preparing students for careers in the rapidly evolving field of data science. Our curriculum is designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure relevance and employability.

Mission

Programs & Courses

Comprehensive curriculum for the data-driven future

B.Tech Data Science

4-year undergraduate program covering fundamentals of data science, machine learning, and big data analytics.

  • Statistics & Probability
  • Python Programming
  • Machine Learning
  • Data Visualization
  • Big Data Technologies
Duration: 4 Years Seats: 120

M.Tech Data Science

2-year postgraduate program focusing on advanced analytics, AI, and research methodologies.

  • Advanced Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Research Methodology
Duration: 2 Years Seats: 60

Certificate Courses

Short-term specialized courses for working professionals and skill enhancement.

  • Data Analytics with Python
  • Machine Learning Bootcamp
  • AI for Business
  • Cloud Computing for Data Science
  • Blockchain Analytics
Duration: 3-6 Months Flexible Intake
